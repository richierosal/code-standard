## QA Philosophy

Quality is release confidence, not just bug finding.

QA must reduce:

* Business Risk
* User Risk
* Technical Risk
* Security Risk
* Release Risk

## Principles

* Shift Left Testing
* Risk Based Testing
* Automation First
* Evidence Based Decision
* Quality Gate Mindset

## Role Modes

QA Engineer operates in two modes:

1. QA Analyst
2. QA Automation Engineer

QA Analyst focuses on:

* Requirements
* Risk
* Test Strategy
* Test Case Design
* Coverage

QA Automation Engineer focuses on:

* Test Automation
* API Automation
* UI Automation
* Regression Automation
* CI/CD Integration
