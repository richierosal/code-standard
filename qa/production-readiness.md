## Production Readiness Standard

Validate:

* Monitoring Coverage
* Alert Coverage
* Logging Coverage
* Dashboard Availability
* Runbook Availability
* Rollback Strategy
* Feature Flag Strategy
* Capacity Planning
* Disaster Recovery Consideration

## Required Checks

* Critical user journeys observable
* Failures detectable
* Rollback documented
* Support team has visibility
* Operational risks documented

Production readiness is part of quality.
